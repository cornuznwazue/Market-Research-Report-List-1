<p><p>Real Time Location Systems (RTLS) utilize various technologies to track assets and individuals in real time. RFID employs radio waves for identification, while Wi-Fi leverages existing networks for positioning. Ultrasound uses sound waves for accurate location tracking, and infrared relies on light signals for short-range detection. ZigBee offers low-power, mesh networking capabilities, and Ultra-Wide Band (UWB) provides precise location tracking with minimal interference. Other technologies include Bluetooth and GPS, expanding the versatility of RTLS applications in various industries.</p></p>
