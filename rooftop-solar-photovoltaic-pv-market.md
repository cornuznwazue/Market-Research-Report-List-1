<p><ul><li>Mono-Si Modules</li><li>Multi-Si Modules</li><li>Thin Film Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rooftop solar photovoltaic (PV) market comprises three main types of modules: Mono-Si, Multi-Si, and Thin Film. Mono-Si modules, known for high efficiency and space efficiency, are made from single-crystal silicon. Multi-Si modules utilize multiple silicon crystals, offering a cost-effective solution but generally lower efficiency. Thin Film modules are made from layers of photovoltaic materials, providing flexibility and lightweight design but typically have lower efficiency compared to crystalline options. Each type suits different applications and budget considerations.</p></p>
