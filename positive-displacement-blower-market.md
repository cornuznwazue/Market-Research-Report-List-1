Medium Pressure Blowers, ranging from 1000 Pa to 3000 Pa, are utilized in industries like pneumatic conveying and wastewater treatment, where moderate pressure is essential. High Pressure Blowers, operating between 3000 Pa and 15000 Pa, serve applications that demand higher pressures, such as industrial vacuum systems and combustion enhancement.</p></p>
