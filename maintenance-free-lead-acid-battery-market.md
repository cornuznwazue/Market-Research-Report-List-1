<p><ul><li>Valve Regulated Type</li><li>Rechargeable Sealed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The maintenance-free lead-acid battery market primarily consists of two types: Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries and Rechargeable Sealed Lead Acid (RSLA) batteries. VRLA batteries, equipped with pressure-regulating valves, prevent gas escape, requiring no electrolyte addition. Conversely, RSLA batteries are designed for high reliability and extended life, often used in applications needing deep cycling. Both types are valued for their low maintenance and versatile applications, making them popular in industries like telecommunications, renewable energy, and automotive.</p></p>
