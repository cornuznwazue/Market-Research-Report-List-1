<p><p>Pneumatic die grinders are versatile tools used in various industries, categorized mainly into two types: angle grinders and straight grinders. Angle grinders feature a rotating head positioned at a right angle to the body, ideal for working in tight spaces and providing excellent maneuverability. Straight grinders have a longer and straight body design, making them suitable for precision tasks and intricate detailing. Both types utilize compressed air, offering powerful performance for grinding, polishing, and shaping various materials.</p></p>
