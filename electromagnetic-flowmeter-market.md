<p><p>The electromagnetic flowmeter market comprises various types tailored to different applications. Inline magnetic flowmeters measure volumetric flow directly in a continuous pipe and are ideal for larger pipelines. Low flow magnetic flowmeters are designed for measuring small flow rates, ensuring precision in applications with low velocity fluids. Insertion magnetic flowmeters are versatile, allowing installation in existing pipes without modifications, making them suitable for a range of industries. Each type provides reliable, accurate flow measurement in diverse operational environments.</p></p>
