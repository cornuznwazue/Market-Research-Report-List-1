Corded portable worklights are powered by electricity, providing consistent brightness and are ideal for extended use in fixed locations. They are often more powerful but limit mobility due to the need for a power source. Conversely, cordless portable worklights are battery-operated, offering greater flexibility and ease of use in varied environments. These are perfect for on-the-go tasks where electricity access is limited, enhancing convenience and portability.</p></p>
