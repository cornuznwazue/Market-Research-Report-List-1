<p><strong>The Digital Die Cutters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade Cutting</li><li>Laser Cutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital die cutters market is primarily divided into two types: blade cutting and laser cutting. Blade cutting utilizes sharp blades to cut through various materials, offering precision and speed for thicker substrates like card stock and foam boards. In contrast, laser cutting employs focused laser beams to achieve intricate designs with minimal material waste, making it suitable for delicate materials, including fabric and paper. Both methods cater to diverse industries, enhancing creativity in packaging, prototyping, and customized products.</p></p>
