<p><strong>The Synchronous Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Excitation Synchronous Motor</li><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synchronous machine market is primarily segmented into electric excitation synchronous motors and permanent magnet synchronous motors. Electric excitation synchronous motors use external power supplies to energize the rotor windings, offering adjustable speed and good performance in various applications. In contrast, permanent magnet synchronous motors use embedded magnets for rotor excitation, providing higher efficiency and compact designs, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and reliability. Both types are integral to modern industrial automation and energy systems, driving advancements in efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
